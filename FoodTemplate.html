Indian Food

Chicken Biryani is a rice dish that can incorporate many different types of spices and foods. It's been a staple for Indian homemade cooking for the whole family to enjoy.

Naan is a flatbread that can be used as a base of many dishes or can take the plate itself covered with butter and herbs. Naan has been used in many Indian dishes to combine with curries and foods.

Dosa is a thin rice batter that is baked into a roudn oval shape resembling an elongated pancake. This dish can be eaten with an assortment of different foods along with chutney and sambar.

Samosas are a classic pastry that is fried with a tasty insise which can be anywhere from a vegetable mix to a non-veg delight. It is a great snack for people all over the world if you can tolderate the spice!
